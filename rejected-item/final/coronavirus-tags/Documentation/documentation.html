Welcome to the Coronavirus-Tags Plugin documentation! Coronavirus-Tags Plugin is a great looking WordPress plugin, that allows you to easily create shortcode. This plugin strong points are flexibility, performance, unique design , Cards, widgets, and live statistics for covid 19

How to Install WordPress Plugins ?

Plugins are utilities which provide additional functionality to your application. To install a plugin you just need to put the plugin files into the wp-content/plugins directory. Once a plugin is installed, you may activate it or deactivate it from the Plugins menu in your WP administration.

  1. Download the plugin to your computer, then log in to your WP admin area and go to Plugins > Add New.
  2. Click On "upload Plugin" button and select "Coronavirus-tags.zip"
  3. Once a plugin is installed, you may activate it

Create Shortcode

A shortcode lets you do nifty things with very little effort. Think of a shortcode as a shortcut to add features to your website that would normally require lots of complicated computer code and technical ability. in this section we are giving you the ability to custom and create your own shortcode for coivd 19 by follow the next steps

  1. Go to admin panel > Covid19-Tags
  2. Click on "Shortcode Builder" and go ahead :)
  3. You custom and create it then copy it from the right side under "shortcode content"

Create Widget

WordPress widgets make it easy for you to add additional functions to your website through a simple drag-and-drop interface. Our plugin also support widget field , its easy just follow the next steps

  1. Go to admin panel > Appearance > Widgets
  2. You just need to drag drop "text widget"
  3. Its up to you regarding set title in widget title but you've to copy paste your own shortcode of Coronavirus-tags shortcode builder in " Text Box"
